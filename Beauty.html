Oscars 2018: Beauty

by Lisa Nevin | 5th Mar 2018

Whilst the Oscars red carpet is often a breeding ground for new beauty trends to emerge, this year's event saw Hollywood's finest each choosing the looks that most summed up their personal style. For Frances McDormand that meant her signature bare-faced beauty, for Lupita Nyong'o an elaborate gold headpiece and for Jennifer Lawrence the smoky eyes and voluminous curls that have come to define her style of late. Saiorse Ronan and Margot Robbie both debuted new bob hairstyles, whilst berry-toned lipstick proved popular with Elisabeth Moss, Salma Hayek and Gal Gadot. See the best looks below.
